Ratan Naval Tata

born 28 December 1937

"The Titan of Industry"

Ratan Tata is an Indian businessman, investor, and philanthropist, who served as the chairman of Tata Sons, the holding company of the Tata Group, from 1991 to 2012. He was born on December 28, 1937, in Bombay, British India (now Mumbai, India). Under his leadership, the Tata Group grew significantly, expanding into new industries and global markets. Ratan Tata is known for his business acumen, ethical leadership, and philanthropic efforts. He has received numerous awards and honors for his contributions to business and society.
